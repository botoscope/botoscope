## What is Botoscope?

Botoscope turns your WordPress + WooCommerce store into a full-featured Telegram shop. Your customers browse your catalog, filter products, place orders, book appointments and pay — all without leaving Telegram. You stay in control: no marketplace rules, no commission cuts.

**Launch your Telegram store in 15 minutes.** Install the plugin, connect your Telegram bot, and you're live.

---

## Features

### 📅 Built-in Booking System
Perfect for beauty salons, consultants, fitness trainers, lawyers, dentists, tutors, and any service business.
- Weekly schedule templates — set your availability once
- Dynamic pricing per time slot — charge more for evening appointments
- Group bookings with capacity control
- Manual reservations for walk-ins

### 🎨 Variable Products
Full WooCommerce variable product support with smooth Telegram UX.
- Multiple attributes (size, color, material, any custom attribute)
- Individual pricing and stock tracking per variation
- Variation images inside Telegram
- Variable items inside grouped bundles

### 💳 Payment Methods
- ⭐ Telegram Stars — built-in Telegram currency, zero redirects
- Stripe — credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- PayPal
- LiqPay
- CryptoBot & CoinGate — crypto payments inside Telegram
- Bank Transfer
- ❤️ PayForMe — let someone else pay via a shareable link

### 🛍️ Product Types
Physical products, services, digital downloads, protected media (video/audio plays inside Telegram, no download), affiliate products, grouped bundles at fixed price.

### 📱 Business in Your Pocket
Get instant Telegram notifications for new orders, customer messages, and low stock alerts. Reply directly from your personal Telegram.

### 🎯 Promotions & Discounts
Formula-based campaigns (e.g. `c>=2; 100%; mc`), percentage or fixed discounts, category targeting, test mode before going live, and standard WooCommerce coupons.

### 🆘 Customer Support Tickets
Built-in ticket system — customers tap "Manager" next to any product or order, you handle it from WordPress admin, replies delivered instantly in Telegram.

### 🌍 Multilingual & Multi-Currency
7 languages built-in: English, Spanish, Ukrainian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Kazakh. Multi-currency via [FOX Currency Switcher](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-currency-switcher/). AI auto-translation with your own OpenAI key.

### 🔍 Product Filters
Advanced filtering inside Telegram via [HUSKY – Products Filter](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-products-filter/) integration.

### 📢 Broadcasting
Send announcements to every customer who has ever started your Telegram shop — no email lists, no spam filters.

## Installation

1. Install via WordPress admin → Plugins → Add New, or download from [wordpress.org/plugins/botoscope](https://wordpress.org/plugins/botoscope/)
2. Activate the plugin
3. Create a Telegram bot via [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather) and copy your bot token
4. Register your shop at [@botoscope_bot](https://t.me/botoscope_bot)
5. Connect the plugin using credentials from your Botoscope dashboard
6. Your Telegram shop is live 🎉

Full guide: [botoscope.com/docs/installing-botoscope-on-your-woocommerce-store](https://botoscope.com/docs/installing-botoscope-on-your-woocommerce-store/)
